SubTrackerPro

Export Data

SubTracker Pro allows you to export your subscription data in multiple formats for easy record keeping and analysis.

Export Options

  • CSV Export: Export all your subscription data to a CSV file that can be opened in Excel or Google Sheets
  • PDF Report: Generate a professional PDF report with charts and summaries
  • JSON Data: Export raw data in JSON format for developers or advanced users

How to Export

  1. Navigate to the dashboard
  2. Click on the "Export" button in the top navigation
  3. Choose your preferred export format
  4. Select the date range and categories to include
  5. Click "Export" and save the file to your device

Benefits

  • Keep offline backups of your subscription data
  • Share reports with accountants or financial advisors
  • Analyze spending patterns over time
  • Import data into other financial management tools

Payment History

Track all your subscription payments and view detailed transaction history to better understand your spending patterns.

Payment Tracking Features

  • Automatic Payment Logging: Payments are automatically recorded when you add or update subscriptions
  • Manual Payment Entry: Add manual payments for subscriptions paid outside the app
  • Payment Status: Track whether payments are pending, completed, or failed
  • Payment Method Tracking: Record which payment method was used for each transaction

Viewing Payment History

Access your payment history through the dashboard:

  • Click on any subscription to view its payment history
  • Filter payments by date range, amount, or payment method
  • Sort payments by date, amount, or status
  • View detailed transaction information including reference numbers

Payment Analytics

  • Monthly Spending: See how much you spend on subscriptions each month
  • Payment Trends: Identify patterns in your payment behavior
  • Budget Tracking: Compare actual spending against your budget
  • Payment Reminders: Get notified before upcoming payments

Share Reports

Easily share your subscription spending reports with family members, roommates, or colleagues to improve financial transparency.

Sharing Options

  • Email Reports: Send detailed reports directly to email addresses
  • Share Links: Generate secure links that can be shared with anyone
  • Export and Share: Export reports and share files through your preferred method
  • Scheduled Sharing: Automatically send reports on a regular schedule

Customizable Reports

Create personalized reports for different recipients:

  • Select specific categories or subscriptions to include
  • Choose date ranges for the report period
  • Add custom notes or messages to reports
  • Customize the appearance and format of reports

Privacy Controls

  • Selective Sharing: Choose exactly what information to share
  • Password Protection: Add password protection to shared reports
  • Expiration Dates: Set expiration dates for shared links
  • Access Tracking: See who has viewed your shared reports

SubTrackerPro

Custom Categories

Create and manage custom categories to better organize your subscriptions according to your personal needs and preferences.

Category Management

  • Create New Categories: Add unlimited custom categories for your subscriptions
  • Edit Existing Categories: Modify category names, colors, and descriptions
  • Delete Categories: Remove categories you no longer need (subscriptions will be reassigned)
  • Category Colors: Assign custom colors to each category for better visual organization

Advanced Features

  • Category Budgets: Set monthly budgets for each category
  • Category Tags: Add tags for more detailed filtering and searching
  • Category Hierarchy: Create subcategories for more detailed organization
  • Bulk Assignment: Assign multiple subscriptions to categories at once

How to Manage Categories

  1. Go to Settings > Categories
  2. Click "Add New Category" to create a custom category
  3. Enter category name, choose a color, and add description
  4. Assign subscriptions to your new category
  5. Use your custom categories in reports and filters
